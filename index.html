Welcome to a website of Dr. Jin BENIYAMA

Hello / Bonjour / こんにちは

I am Jin Beniyama, a postdoc at Observatoire de la Côte d’Azur (OCA). I have mainly engaged in observations of near-Earth asteroids using Japanese telescope, and I am now studying the thermal properties of asteroids via ThermoPhysical Modeling (TPM). Please feel free to contact me (mail:jbeniyama[at]oca.eu) if you are interested in my studies!
